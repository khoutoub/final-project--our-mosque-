About Halal Mosque

Our History

Founded in 2007, Halal Mosque has been a beacon of faith and community in Indianapolis IN. What started as a small gathering of believers in a rented hall has blossomed into a vibrant community center that welcomes hundreds of worshippers each week. Over the years, we have expanded our facilities and programs to meet the growing needs of our diverse community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for all individuals seeking knowledge and understanding of Islam. We aim to foster a community that supports spiritual growth, charitable deeds, and interfaith dialogue. Through education, worship, and service, we commit to contributing positively to the broader society.

Leadership

Our mosque is guided by Imam Ahmed Al-Farsi, a respected scholar and community leader with over 20 years of experience. Supported by a dedicated team of volunteers and professionals, our leadership strives to uphold the principles of Islam while addressing the contemporary needs of our community.

Community Work

Halal Mosque actively engages in various community service projects, including a food pantry for the needy, educational programs for children and adults, and interfaith dialogue initiatives. Our annual charity drive supports both local and international humanitarian causes, demonstrating our commitment to compassion and global brotherhood.
