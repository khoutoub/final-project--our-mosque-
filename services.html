Community Services

We are dedicated to serving our community through various programs and services:

  • Education: We offer classes on Islamic studies, Arabic language, and more.
  • Outreach: Regular visits to hospitals and senior homes are part of our outreach program.
  • Support Services: Counseling and support for individuals and families in need.
