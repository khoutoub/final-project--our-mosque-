Community Services

At Sahaba Mosque, we're committed to serving not just our congregation, but our local community at large. Here are some of the services we offer:

  • Food Pantry: Open every Wednesday from 3 PM to 5 PM. Providing essential groceries to those in need.
  • Educational Programs: Free classes on Islamic studies, Arabic language, and more for children and adults.
  • Counseling Services: Offering spiritual, family, and youth counseling by appointment.
  • Community Outreach: Regular volunteer opportunities including neighborhood clean-ups, feeding the homeless, and interfaith dialogues.

To learn more or get involved, please reach out to us.
