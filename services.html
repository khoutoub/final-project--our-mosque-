Community Services and Education

Halal Mosque is committed to serving the community through a variety of services and educational programs:

  • Food Bank: Providing essential groceries to those in need every Saturday from 10 AM to 1 PM.
  • After-school Tutoring: Free tutoring services for children in mathematics, science, and Arabic language.
  • Marriage Counseling: Offering guidance and counseling for couples by appointment.

To learn more about our services or to volunteer, please contact us.
