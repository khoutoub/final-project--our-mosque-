Educational Programs

At Sahaba Mosque, we are committed to providing comprehensive Islamic education for all age groups. Our programs are designed to foster a deep understanding of the Islamic faith, teachings of the Quran, and the Arabic language. Below are some of the programs we offer:

  • Children's Quran Classes: Tailored for ages 5-12, focusing on Quranic recitation, memorization, and basic Islamic teachings.
  • Adult Arabic Language Courses: Beginner to advanced levels to help adults fully comprehend the Quran and Hadith.
  • Islamic Studies for Teens: Engaging sessions that explore Islamic history, ethics, and contemporary issues relevant to Muslim youth.

For more information on schedules and registration, please visit our contact page or reach out directly through our provided contact information.
